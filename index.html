How we met

Our love story is proof that fate has a wild sense of humor.

The first time we met? At a running contest. Sparks flew, but no words did—probably because Phong (the groom) was too busy battling a heroic stomach ache.

The second encounter? A hotspot party. Sounds cool, right? Except we somehow ended up on opposite sides of the venue, unable to see or talk to each other. Smooth, huh?

But third time’s the charm. On a hiking trip, we finally got it right. No running, no wrong sides—just us, realizing we were the one for each other. We knew right then and there: no more missed chances.
